Amazon Sales Visualizer

Real-Time Business Intelligence Dashboard (2025)

As an Amazon seller running Atlatl & Co, I needed better visibility into my business metrics. Amazon's Seller Central provides data, but it's spread across multiple pages and lacks the visual clarity needed for quick decision-making. I built a custom sales visualization dashboard to aggregate and display key business metrics in real-time.

The Challenge

Running a product business on Amazon generates extensive data: sales volume, revenue, profit margins, inventory levels, advertising spend, and customer metrics. However, Amazon's native interface makes it difficult to see trends at a glance or correlate different metrics. I needed a tool that could answer questions like "How is my new product performing compared to last month?" or "What's my actual profit after all fees and advertising costs?"
