Force Multipliers for Modern Life

The atlatl is an ancient spear-throwing tool that extends human capability—transforming the throwing motion into a force multiplier that increases range and power. Similarly, Atlatl & Co develops products that amplify what people can achieve in their daily lives.

Our Philosophy

We identify specific problems where thoughtful engineering and design can create meaningful improvements. Each product combines technical innovation with practical utility, developed through iterative refinement and real-world testing.

Our approach leverages advanced manufacturing techniques—particularly 3D printing—to serve niche markets with custom solutions that larger manufacturers overlook. This allows us to deliver specialized products at reasonable prices while maintaining the flexibility to iterate based on customer feedback.

SNOO Smart Sleeper Mobility Wheels (v1)

The SNOO bassinet is an innovative product for new parents, but its 38-pound weight makes it difficult to move between rooms. Our retractable wheel system transforms it into an easily mobile unit, allowing parents to keep their baby close throughout the day without the burden of lifting and carrying.

The system features retractable wheels that deploy when needed and retract to maintain stability during use. Designed specifically for the SNOO's dimensions and weight distribution, the wheels provide smooth mobility while maintaining safety and stability.

Customer Feedback: While many customers love the v1 wheels for their quick installation and effective mobility, we've also received valuable feedback about design and quality concerns. We take this feedback seriously and have used it to develop our v2 model.
